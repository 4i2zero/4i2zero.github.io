Visionary Indian Music Producer

Who is 4i2zero?

Tushar Gaur, known professionally as 4i2zero, is a visionary Indian music producer. His artistry challenges traditional notions of genre and region. As a champion of the drill sound, his music exemplifies an exhilarating new era where artists can build worldwide audiences with niche sounds, unbound by conventional definitions of success.

His Sonic Landscape

  • The Low End as Heartbeat: His beats are pulsating and thunderous. The relentless low-end frequencies are an integral part of drill music, creating an undercurrent of tension and drive vital to the genre.
  • A Touch of the Unexpected: Within this menacing framework, 4i2zero weaves in surprising sonic elements. Traditional Indian instruments like the sitar or tabla often find their space amidst the heavy electronic beats. The resulting sound is both familiar and jarringly fresh.
  • Atmosphere as Mood: 4i2zero understands that a beat isn't just about rhythm. He deftly builds atmosphere with haunting strings, dissonant synths, and sound effects, ensuring that his music isn't just felt but also experienced.

A Pioneer in More Ways Than One

  • The Rise of the Global South: 4i2zero underscores the emergence of music scenes outside the traditional Western hubs. He is proof that cultural influences can flow organically and rapidly in the internet age, inspiring artists worldwide.
  • Evolution of Hip-Hop: Drill, often associated with cities like Chicago and London, is finding fertile ground in India thanks to producers like 4i2zero. This underscores the global adaptability and ever-evolving nature of hip-hop.
